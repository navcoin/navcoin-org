NavCoin's Features

Sustainable Proof of Stake

NavCoin runs on PoS, which consumes a fraction of the energy of a Proof of Work network.

Network Scalability

Testing shows current network capacity at 260+ tx per second - alongside research in layer 1 and 2 scaling solutions.

Low Cost Global Payments

Send money cheaply anywhere in the world without needing to go through a bank

Privacy

Privacy has always been a central part of NavCoin, from the original NavTech to the upcoming zNAV

Quick Payments

Transactions show up in seconds, and confirmed within 30 seconds, makes it ideal for commerce.

Pay a name, not a number

NavCoin uses OpenAlias to make sending money as easy as typing in an email address.

Why NavCoin

Values Built Into The Network

  • Open & Transparent Governance

    No one is in control - everyone is free to work on NavCoin however they want. There are no forced protocol changes, as every wallet gets a say in the direction of NavCoin.

  • Open Source

    Open Source is the backbone for NavCoin. Every line of code is viewable, and anyone can contribute to the website. Building trust through transparency.

  • Be your own bank

    Being a peer to peer network, means that there are no banks or intermediaries getting in the way. Take back control - your vote counts

  • Building a new system

    NavCoin is symbol of change for how the entire global economic system works - one that returns freedom, privacy and financial power back to the individual.

  • Fair, Clean & Green

    Individuals don’t live in bubbles, everyone affects the world in different ways. This is why NavCoin strives to be energy efficient in order to do good for our planet.

  • Privacy

    Privacy is about choice - giving you control of your personal information and who you share it with
