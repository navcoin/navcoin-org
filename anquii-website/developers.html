Developers

Navcoin and its ecosystem is built by people from all around the world, and with transparency at its heart. Every line of code can be viewed, and anyone can contribute or reuse what we've built. No matter the skill level, everyone has the potential to make a positive impact on Navcoin and the goals that we all share of building a better tomorrow.

Ways to contribute

There are many ways for developers of all skill levels and expertise to contribute to Navcoin.

Submit a NPIP

If you have any suggestions on how to improve the protocol of Navcoin's network, then we welcome you to submit a Navcoin Protocol Improvement Proposal (NPIP).

Learn more

Write or review code

Anyone is welcome to contribute towards the development of Navcoin's network and its associated products in the form of peer review, testing, patches and features.

Learn more

Claim a bounty

Participate in one of our bounty programs and earn a reward for contributions such as finding a bug in our software, or by fixing an existing one.

Learn more

Apply for a grant

Any developer can apply for a grant to be issued by our network through its decentralized treasury as payment for their contributions to Navcoin and its ecosystem.

Learn more

Acknowledgement

Thank you to those that have made the Navcoin protocol a reality, to those that have contributed to it at one point or another, and to those that continues to move it forward on a daily basis.
